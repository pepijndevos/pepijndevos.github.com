I copied their comments.php over to my theme, but unfortunately this only made matter worse. It uses a few functions defined in functions.php. Rather than trying to copy those specific functions over to my functions.php, I just included theirs: 
include(TEMPLATEPATH . "/../twentyten/functions.php");
